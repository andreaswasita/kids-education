ğŸ’¡ Golden Rules â€” Read This Every Week

1
Never force it. If he's tired or frustrated, stop. Try again tomorrow.
2
Keep it under 15 minutes per activity. Short bursts beat long slogs.
3
Read aloud every single night. Non-negotiable. Highest-impact activity.
4
Play IS learning. LEGO, puzzles, blocks, and board games all count.
5
Talk to him constantly. Narrate your day, ask questions, expand vocabulary.
6
Be consistent, not intense. 8 hrs/week gentle practice > 3 hrs weekend cramming.
7
Celebrate everything. First word read? Party. Counted to 20? High five.
8
Praise effort, not smarts. "You worked so hard!" not "You're so smart!"
9
Let him struggle a little. Productive struggle builds resilience.
10
No screens as babysitter. Educational apps need parent involvement at this age.
