ğŸ’ª Confidence-Building Rules
  1. Start with what she CAN do. Begin each session with 2-3 easy questions to build confidence.
  2. Praise effort, not results. "You worked so hard on that!" not "You got it right!"
  3. Mistakes are data, not failures. Wrong answers tell us what to learn next.
  4. Make it real. Cooking, shopping, measuring â€” maths is everywhere.
  5. Never say "I was bad at maths too." Maths anxiety is learned. Keep it positive.
  6. Use Khan Academy videos for topics she finds hard. Watch the 5 min video, then practice.
  7. Track progress visually. Use IXL skill scores. Celebrate when a topic hits 80%+.
  8. Stop after 30 min. Even if she hasn't finished. Maths fatigue makes things worse.
