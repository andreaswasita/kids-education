## Reading Challenge Ideas

### Monthly Reading Bingo
Create a 5Ã—5 bingo card with categories:
- A book set in Australia
- A non-fiction book
- A book with a number in the title
- A book by an author you've never read
- A book recommended by a friend
- A graphic novel
- A book about science
- A book from a series
- A biography
- A book published this year

### The 100 Books Challenge
Track toward reading 100 books in a year (that's about 2 per week â€” very achievable for a keen reader!)

### Family Book Club
Pick a book to read together. Discuss it over dinner:
- What surprised you?
- Which character would you want to be?
- Would you change the ending?
- Rate it out of 5 stars
