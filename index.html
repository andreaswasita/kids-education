üí° Golden Rules

  1. Never force it. If he's tired or frustrated, stop. Try again tomorrow.
  2. Keep it under 15 minutes per activity. Short bursts beat long slogs.
  3. Read aloud every single night. Non-negotiable. Highest-impact activity.
  4. Play IS learning. LEGO, puzzles, blocks, and board games all count.
  5. Talk to him constantly. Narrate your day, ask questions, expand vocabulary.
  6. Be consistent, not intense. 8 hrs/week gentle practice > 3 hrs weekend cramming.
  7. Celebrate everything. First word read? Party. Counted to 20? High five.
