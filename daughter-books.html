PEEL Paragraph Structure

Point â€” State your argument

Explain â€” Why this evidence matters

Body 1: Strongest argument (PEEL)

Body 2: Second argument (PEEL)

Body 3: Counter-argument + rebuttal

Conclusion: Restate position + call to action

ğŸ“ Practice Prompts

Persuasive Prompts

#PromptTip
1Should homework be banned?Think about both sides first, then choose one.
2Should students wear school uniforms?Use personal experience as evidence.
3Are zoos good or bad for animals?Include a counter-argument and rebuttal.
4Should children have mobile phones?Think about safety AND distraction arguments.
5Should plastic bags be banned completely?Use facts and statistics if you can.
6Is it better to read books or watch movies?Use specific examples from books you've read.

Narrative Prompts

#PromptTip
1Write about a time you found something unexpected.Start with action, not "One day..."
2You wake up and discover you can fly.Use sensory details â€” what do you see, hear, feel?
3A mysterious letter arrives at your door.Build suspense slowly. Don't reveal everything at once.
4Write about a journey that changed everything.Focus on the character's emotions, not just events.
5You find a door that leads to another world.Describe the new world using all five senses.
6The last day of something important.Use flashbacks to build emotional depth.
